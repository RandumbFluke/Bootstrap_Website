About Us

A community service which is here to promote the wellbeing of the environment and increase the health of our community. We as the Bristol community have decided to scatter multiple bikes around the city and with a few main hub locations in major locations, this will allow for multiple sight-seeing experiences of our great city while spreading a new culture to our amazing community. We hope this will encourage our audience to become more eco-friendly and stray away from polluting vehicles.

How it Works

To hire a bike, you must first become a member, learn more by pressing the button! Either using our reservation form or finding a bike on the map. Once at a bike pay to unlock the bike and then ride away, however all bikes must be returned to a designated spot, shown on the map.
